Frequently Asked Questions.

Got questions about how BunkSafe calculates attendance or syncs with iCloud? We have you covered.

BunkSafe uses a real-time mathematical formula comparing your total attended lectures against total conducted lectures. It determines the maximum number of future classes you can miss consecutively without causing your overall attendance percentage to fall below your specified requirement target (e.g., 75%).
Yes! Leveraging Apple's latest WidgetKit framework, BunkSafe interactive widgets allow you to tap "Present" or "Absent" directly from your Home Screen or Lock Screen. The app's background engine immediately updates your attendance logs and recalculates your Safe Bunks instantly.
When you enable iCloud sync in BunkSafe settings, your attendance logs, schedule, and exam dates are stored securely inside your personal Apple iCloud container using Apple's native CloudKit technology. Your data syncs automatically across your iPhone, iPad, and Mac without requiring a third-party server account.
